About Me

A Java and Full Stack developer graduated from Wellington Institute of Technology with a Bachelor of Information Technology. Loves following ground breaking engineering and technology advancement around the world. Always busy learning something everyday.

Growing up in New Zealand with a fascination in computers and software development, I pursued after a formal education and graduated from Wellington Institute of Technology with a Bachelor of Information Technology.

Engaging in problem solving to solve current world and business problems with IT is what I love about software development. I have applied my skills including Java and Full Stack to many groups and organisation to help solve unique IT challenges, ranging from data processing tools for Semantic Web to a Full Stack Metadata Visualisation tool to enhance the value of an organisation's data vault.

During my career break, I have continued to engage my problem solving and learning in many advance IT fields such as Semantic Graph with my open source projects. Such endeavors have matured the skills I currently have as well as the chance to pick up new invaluable knowledge and skills in open data and software automation that I can bring to a team.

Resuming my career in IT, I am excited in continuing the journey ahead in securing a position as a software developer. I am currently studying in multiple courses in Front End, Python and ITSQB Testing with the aim of enhancing my expertise as a full stack developer.

My career interest lies with an innovative company that values building better products for consumers through Team building and continuous learning. I always look forward to bringing the skills and expertise gained from previous experience to add to these values and grow with them even more.
